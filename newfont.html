TALES ARTE

EVERY YEAR I LIKE TO CREATE A NEW FONT FOR THE TAGS OF THE SEASON. MAYBE I’M HOMESICK BECAUSE IN 2023 I’VE DECIDED TO CREATE ONE FONT INSPIRED BY THE GRAFFITI OF PORTO ALEGRE AND SAO PAULO.

I DESIGNED IT TO BE RAW AND DISRUPTIVE, MY MAIN GOAL WAS TO BRING BRUTALISM AND THE URBAN SPACE OF THE GLOBAL SOUTH TO DIFFERENT PLATFORMS AND WIDE CONTEXT.

AS A FONT CREATED FOR TAGGING, I HAD TO WORK ON A FEW LINES THAT CAN EASILY BE PAINTED USING ROLL PAINT TECHNIQUE.
